Nightmare Alley is an upcoming American psychological thriller film directed by Guillermo del Toro with a screenplay by del Toro and Kim Morgan, based on the 1946 novel of the same name by William Lindsay Gresham. The film features an ensemble cast that includes Bradley Cooper, Cate Blanchett, Toni Collette, Willem Dafoe, Richard Jenkins, Rooney Mara, Ron Perlman, and David Strathairn. It is the second feature film adaptation of Gresham's novel, following the 1947 version.

Nightmare Alley will hold its world premiere at the Academy Museum of Motion Pictures in Los Angeles in late 2021, and is scheduled to be released in the United States on December 17, 2021, by Searchlight Pictures.
