Hometown Cha-Cha-Cha is a 2021 South Korean television series starring Shin Min-a, Kim Seon-ho and Lee Sang-yi. It is a remake of 2004 South Korean film Mr. Handy, Mr. Hong. It aired from August 28 to October 17, 2021 on tvN's Saturdays and Sundays at 21:00 (KST) time slot. It is also available internationally for streaming on Netflix.

The series was a commercial hit and became one of the highest-rated series on cable television history.[8][9] It ranked first place during its run for eight weeks, and the last episode achieved 12.665% nationwide rating, with over 3.2 million views.
